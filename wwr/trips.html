What to Expect

Plenty of adventure awaits on any one of these trips! You can expect to spend anywhere between 2 to 6 days depending on the route you choose and on your own pace. Before all customers set voyage, they will be provided with a list of supplies to bring. You will have to check your packs with instructors and with your guide prior to beginning.
